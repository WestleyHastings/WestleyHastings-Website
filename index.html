Pre Business

My name is Westley Hastings and I play soccer at UC Riverside. I was born in Fredericksburg, Virginia and bounced around the country for the next 12 years with a military family. I drove through every state in the United states while doing so which was an amazing experience. Throughout this time I was playing soccer. Playing with people from different states was great because I got to play people that played a different style of soccer. After twelve years of bouncing around the states, my family and I settled down in Oceanside, California. Oceanside, California was the most beautiful city I had been in so far and I couldn’t wait to live out the rest of my youth there.

I made many amazing friends that will probably be at my wedding, as well as me being at theirs. For soccer, I played for Oceanside Breakers inthe heart of Oceanside, about5 minutes from the beach. Oceanside Breakers was the local club so I was drawn to it because of the name being the same name as the city I was in. Every year I spent with the team I loved more than life it self. I got a lot better over the course of the next 6 years and was enjoying the sport immensely.

I also started playing for a local professional soccer academy with some of my closest friends. Doing so I also made many life long friends that I still talk to to this day. Now, my life consists of waking up at eight in the morning, going to class, going to soccer practice from 8 to 1:30, and then going to class until 5. Although it is very tiring, I do enjoy it. I love being on a team that feels like a family and also a team that wins. It makes the rest of the day and my classes go by quick.
